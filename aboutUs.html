A Brief History

GS Keys

GS Keys was established in May 2019 by George Oddes and Steven Rayd.

The company was set up after identifying that many people were being overcharged, for services rendered.

It appeared that nobody could do a complete job on site without removing parts and in some cases never returning to complete the work. This is where we saw a gap in the market, an opportunity to be different and ahead of the rest.

GS Keys is a complete one stop mobile workshop that comes to its customer's to supply new keys and attend to their every need as quikly, efficiantly and cost effectivly as possible. All work is completed on site, there is no waiting period (unless specific parts need to be ordered) and we strive to assist all our clients in the shortest time frame possible.

GS Keys offers the following services:
  • Cut and code lost or stolen car keys, both remote and non remote keys.
  • Replace locks that have been damaged.
  • Change combination of locks and supplying of new keys/ locks.
  • Supplying of spare keys
  • Servicing of remotes and vehicle keys/ fobs
  • Opening Of Vehicles
Replacement Keys are either:
  • Originals from vehicle manufacturing agents
  • Non original from Automotive locksmith supply SA / Lock trading BBL supply
Collectively we have over 20 years of locksmithing experience, and over 30 years in the motor industry. With electrical qualifications and locksmithing qualifications you can be assured that we will look after you and your vehicle in your time of need.
