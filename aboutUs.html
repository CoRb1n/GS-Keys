A Brief History

GS Keys

GS Keys was started in may 2019 by Myself George Oddes and Steven Rayd.

The company was started off the back of us finding a lot of people were being charged extra, for replacing car keys. it appeared that nobody could do a complete job on site without removing parts and in some cases never returning to complete the the work.

GS Keys is a complete 1 stop mobile workshop that comes to the customer's property and supply new keys ,open cut and code the vehicle. Gskeys comes to the customers premises and all the work is carried out at the same time. Nothing is removed unless really necessary.

Gskeys offers the following services:
  • cut and code lost or stolen car keys, both remote and non remote keys.
  • Replace locks that have been damaged.
  • Change combination of locks and supplying of new keys.
  • The keys that are used for the replacement of keys are either:
    • original from the agent's
    • and non original from Automotive locksmith supply SA
      • Lock trading BBL supply
Collectivly we have over 20 years of locksmithing experience, and over 30 years in the motor industry as a whole. With electrical qualifications and locksmithing qualifications you can be assured that we will look after you and your vehicle in your time of need.
